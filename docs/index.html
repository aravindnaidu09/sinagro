About Sinagro

Our Team

Our team at Sinagro is made up of experienced and knowledgeable professionals who are passionate about agriculture. We work together to provide exceptional service to our clients.

Our Mission

At Sinagro, we are committed to providing high-quality agricultural services that help our clients succeed. We believe in sustainable farming practices and strive to be good stewards of the land.

Our Story

Founded in 2022, Sinagro started as a small family-owned farm. Over the years, we have expanded our services and now offer a wide range of agricultural solutions to businesses in the area.

Our Services

Smart Irrigation

IoT-based smart irrigation helps farmers improve crop yields, save water and energy, and make data-driven decisions. Remote control allows for monitoring via smartphone.

Land Geo Tagging

SINAGRO maps module helps farmers in geo-tagging plots to record accurate details about plot sizes, crop type, crop age, soil health, weather forecast, and more.

Soil Health Report

SINAGRO app provides soil health components such as Nitrogen, Potassium, Phosphorus, organic carbon, and soil pH through remote sensing technology.

Soil Moisture

Generates soil moisture reports to help farmers decide crop types and irrigation management strategies based on soil's water content.

Crop Health

NDVI reports generated every 5 days help farmers pinpoint crop issues, allowing them to focus on areas that need the most attention.

Weather Forecast

SINAGRO app provides weekly and daily weather reports, including wind speed, UV level, humidity, and "feels like" temperature.

Crop Calendar

The crop calendar helps farmers plan farm activities and make decisions about crop practices, from land preparation to harvest.

Pest and Disease Forewarning

Provides forewarning on pests and diseases, helping farmers take timely action with symptoms and management solutions.

Buy and Sell

SINAGRO enables farmers to post their crops for sale, reaching buyers directly and facilitating trade without intermediaries.

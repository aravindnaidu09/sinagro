About Sinagro

Our Team

Our team at Sinagro is made up of experienced and knowledgeable professionals who are passionate about agriculture. We work together to provide exceptional service to our clients.

Our Mission

At Sinagro, we are committed to providing high-quality agricultural services that help our clients succeed. We believe in sustainable farming practices and strive to be good stewards of the land.

Our Story

Founded in 2022, Sinagro started as a small family-owned farm. Over the years, we have expanded our services and now offer a wide range of agricultural solutions to businesses in the area.

Our Services

Smart Irrigation

IoT-based smart irrigation systems that improve crop yields, save water, and reduce the impact of climate change and drought.

Land Geo Tagging

Geo-tagging for accurate agricultural plot mapping, ensuring accurate plot size and crop details.

Soil Health Report

Provides soil health information like Nitrogen, Potassium, Phosphorus, Soil organic carbon, and Soil pH.

Soil Moisture

Generates soil moisture report every 12 days, helping in water management strategies and irrigation decisions.

Crop Calendar

A planning tool that helps farmers with information on sowing rates, planting material, and farming practices.

Crop Health

Generates NDVI report every 5 days to identify crop health and focus on areas needing attention.

Weather Forecast

Provides detailed weather information including wind speed, UV level, humidity, temperature, and forecasts.

Pest and Disease Forewarning

Provides timely information on pests and diseases for crop protection, including symptoms and management solutions.

Buy and Sell

A platform for farmers to post commodities for global reach, facilitating direct trading without intermediaries.
